AtomiFi empowers real estate operators to cost-effectively finance, grow, and monetize their portfolio by tapping into the public capital markets through distinct-asset IPOs. In a distinct-asset real estate IPO, AtomiFi issues shares of both debt and equity backed by a single specific property. These shares comply with US regulations and are registered with SEC. This allows AtomiFi Real Estate Securities to be publicly traded without any restrictions, just like the stock of S&P 500 companies.
